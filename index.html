Recipe #1

  • Time: 75 mins
  • Serves: 10
Recipe Ingredients Steps
Chocolate Cake
  • 1 + 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 + 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 + 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup vegtable oil
  • 1 cup of brewed coffee
    Step 1:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees ferenheit. Oil a spring pan, lining the bottom with parchment paper.

    Step 2: In a bowl, gentely whisk together the dry ingredients.

    Step 3: In either a seprate bowl or a stand mixer, combine the milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla extract. Add coffee slowly ensuring that it's not too hot.

    Step 4: Slowly incorporate the dry mixture into the wet.

    Step 5: Once the batter is cohesive, pour into pan. Place in an oven and bake for 55 minutes. Let the cake cool down before topping with powdered sugar.

    Source: Best Fudgy Chocolate Cake

Recipe #2

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 5
    Recipe Ingredients Steps
    Spicy Shrimp Tacos with Sirracha Slaw
  • 1 pound shrimp
  • 1 tbs Paprika
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 2 cups of mixed shredded cabbage
  • 3 tbs Apple Cider Vinager
  • 1/4 cup Mayonaise
  • 4 tbs Siracha
  • Soft corn tortillas
    		•

      Step 1:

      Shell and rinse shrimp. Drain and move to bowl. Add olive oil and season using paprika, garlic, and pepper(s). Leave to marinate.

      Step 2:

      In a seperate bowl mix shredded cabbage with apple cider and salt. Next incorporate the mayo and sirracha. Add salt and pepper to taste.

      Step 3:

      Heat pan prior to adding shrimp. Once added, cook for 2 minutes on each side, or until the shrimp are curled. Toss with lime and cilantro if desired.

      Step 4:

      Heat up tortillas. Assemble tacos with the shrimp and slaw! Enjoy!

      Source: Spicy Siracha Shrimp Tacos
